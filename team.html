Board of Directors – Mabale Growers Tea Factory

The Board of Directors of Mabale Growers Tea Factory serves as the highest decision-making body, providing strategic leadership and governance to ensure the success of the factory. What makes this board unique is that the owners of the factory are the local tea growers. This farmer-owned model ensures that the factory operates in the best interests of the growers, promoting fair trade, transparency, and sustainable growth. The Board consists of elected representatives from the tea growers, industry experts, and key stakeholders. The Board is responsible for overseeing the factory’s financial health, operational efficiency, and long-term sustainability.

The Genaral Manager – Mabale Growers Tea Factory

The Rise of Mr. Joseph Katembo – The General Manager of Mabale Growers Tea Factory At the helm of operations is Mr. Joseph Katembo, a determined and visionary leader who has taken on the mantle of General Manager at a time when the factory needed fresh energy. A New Leader in Challenging Times Joseph Katembo was no stranger to the tea industry. Having worked in various agricultural and business management roles, he had developed a reputation as a man who understood both the farmers and the market. When he was appointed as the General Manager of Mabale Growers Tea Factory, the industry was facing new challenges—climate change, fluctuating tea prices, and increasing operational costs threatened to slow down production.

Operations Manager – Mabale Growers Tea Factory

Behind the smooth running of the factory is a man whose dedication and expertise keeping everything in motion Mr. Peter Chisale, the Operations Manager. A Man of Precision and Dedication Peter Chisale had always been fascinated by how things worked. He saw firsthand the struggles of tea growers—how their efforts depended on the efficiency of the factory. After studying industrial operations and agribusiness management, he worked in different tea factories before returning to Mabale Growers Tea Factory, determined to make a difference. As Operations Manager, Peter is in charge of ensuring that every stage of tea processing—from receiving fresh tea leaves to packaging the final product—ran smoothly and efficiently.
