Our Story

We are dedicated to producing high-quality tea grown and processed in the heart of our local community. Our sustainable practices and commitment to quality ensure that every cup of tea supports local farmers and delights tea lovers everywhere.

"Crafting the perfect blend sip sip"

Our Vision

To become the leading provider of high-quality and innovative tea products, catering to the diverse tastes and preferences of our customers while upholding environmental sustainability and social responsibility.

Our Mission

Our mission is to source the finest tea leaves from trusted suppliers, utilize state-of-the-art processing techniques to ensure premium quality, and deliver a wide range of delicious and healthy tea products to our customers. We are dedicated to continuous innovation, customer satisfaction, and fostering a culture of sustainability in our operations.

Recent Highlights

On March 5, 2025, over 120 students from Kyambogo University toured our factory, learning about sustainable tea production.

Empowering Local Farmers

At Mabale Growers Tea Factory,Our strength lies in our farmers.
